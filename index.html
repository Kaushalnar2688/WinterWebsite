About Winter

Winter is the coldest season of the year, characterized by snowy days, cozy nights, and festive holidays. From snowball fights to sipping hot cocoa by the fire, winter brings families and friends together.

Winter Activities

  • Build a snowman or have a snowball fight
  • Go ice skating or sledding
  • Bake holiday cookies
  • Decorate your home with Christmas lights
  • Watch holiday movies with hot chocolate

Fun Winter Facts

  • Did you know that no two snowflakes are exactly alike?
  • The world’s largest snowflake was reportedly 15 inches wide!
  • Reindeers’ noses can appear red due to increased blood flow in cold weather.
  • Winter in the Northern Hemisphere lasts from December to February.
